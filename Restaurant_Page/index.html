Our Story

A Culinary Journey Across Two Continents

Founded in 2020, Masala & Mozzarella Café was born from a passion for bringing together the rich, aromatic spices of India with the comforting, elegant flavors of Italy. Our chefs craft each dish with precision, blending traditional techniques with innovative fusion concepts.

We believe that great food brings people together. Our menu features carefully curated dishes that honor both culinary traditions while creating something uniquely delicious. From our signature Butter Chicken Pizza to our Spicy Arrabbiata with a hint of garam masala, every dish tells a story.

Come experience a dining adventure where East meets West, and every meal is a celebration of flavor, culture, and community.
